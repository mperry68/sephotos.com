About Us

Passionate photographers dedicated to capturing your beautiful moments

Our Story

Since 2015, sephoto has been offering a unique and personalized photography experience in Beaconsfield, QC. We are a team of two passionate professionals dedicated to creating the right ambiance to highlight and capture your best moments.

We believe that every moment deserves to be beautifully preserved. Whether it's a family portrait, a newborn's first photos, or a couple's special session, we approach each project with creativity, professionalism, and a genuine passion for photography.

Your only dilemma will be which photo to share first!
